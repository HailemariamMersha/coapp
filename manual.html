Common App Guide

  1. Create an Account: Sign up at commonapp.org
  2. Choose Colleges: Add schools to your application list.
  3. Fill Personal Info: Name, address, family, etc.
  4. Education History: High school, GPA, transcripts.
  5. Activities: List clubs, leadership, and other involvements.
  6. Test Scores: Optional for many schools, but include if required.
  7. Personal Statement: Write the main essay (CoApp helps with this!).
  8. College-Specific Questions: Some schools ask extra questions or essays.
  9. Recommendations: Invite teachers and counselors to submit letters.
  10. Review & Submit: Double-check and submit with fee or waiver.
